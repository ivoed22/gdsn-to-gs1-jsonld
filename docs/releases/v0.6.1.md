# v0.6.1 — Warning Cleanup and Conformance Notes

## Overview

Version 0.6.1 reviews every warning remaining after v0.6.0 and separates safe
reporting fixes from genuine standards and governance decisions.

## Changed

- Removed three structural parent-object false positives from warning counts.
- Preserved those findings as informational, auditable report entries.
- Reduced `check-mapping` warnings from 15 to 12.
- Added `standards_review_required` to quality messages.
- Added validation evidence and category-specific recommended actions.
- Documented all fixed and intentionally retained warnings.

## Preserved

- `check-catalog` remains at 0 errors and 8 non-blocking warnings.
- Converter output is unchanged.
- Mapping YAML files and semantic mappings are unchanged.
- Existing sample conversions remain unchanged.
- No new dependencies were added.

## Remaining Conformance Notes

Twelve non-blocking warnings remain: three nutrient-modelling, two
document/DPP, two Web Vocabulary replacement-term, one image-modelling, and
four YAML/catalog governance warnings. These require standards or governance
review and are not suppressed or presented as resolved.
